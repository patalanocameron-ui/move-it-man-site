MIM–07FAQ
Do you do full-house moves?

No. We specialize in the hard stuff. Safes, pianos, hot tubs, pool tables, and other heavy, high-value items that regular movers turn down. For a full household move, you'll want a full-service moving company.

What kinds of items do you move?

Safes, pianos, pool tables, hot tubs, high-end furniture, POD contents, and Marketplace finds that need pickup and delivery, with assembly if needed.

What's the fastest way to get started?

If it's urgent, call 386-538-1455. Otherwise, use the quote form so we get your pickup, delivery, and access details up front and can quote you in one reply.
